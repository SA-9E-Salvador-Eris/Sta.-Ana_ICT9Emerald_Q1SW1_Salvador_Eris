Calliope

Name: Calliope

Alias/Aliases: Aryan Invader

Inspiration: One of my friends

Short Description: She is one of the 5 war criminals in the school this story is placed within. She is capable of influencing everyone into bidding for her. Only a few people were able to deflect this, one being Ethan.

Abilities: High IQ, Battle IQ

Most Powerful Feat: Went back in time to take over the Indus Valley Civilization and kill hundreds in the process.

Fun Fact: Calliope was originally going to have the initials AA.
