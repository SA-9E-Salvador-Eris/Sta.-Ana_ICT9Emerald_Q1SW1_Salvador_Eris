The Grand Dealmaker

(Left to Right, Image of The Grand Dealmaker, Image of The Grand Dealmaker in Live Action Version)

Name: The Grand Dealmaker

Alias/Aliases: Taxes, TDG, The Grand Punisher

Inspiration: Businessmen/Alastor from Hazbin Hotel

Short Description: A sprit representing punishment and punishes people by seeing if they will take the oppurtunity to take their sinful desires when given the chance

What he can do: Destroy fleets of armies while sitting on a chair drinking tea, cause explosions and fires in an instant, create mass extinctions

Fun Fact: The nickname "Taxes" came from a scene in the pilot where The Grand Dealmaker makes a deal with a guy named Henry with Henry's end of the deal being doing the dealmaker's taxes
